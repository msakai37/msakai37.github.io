Hi, my name is Mina Leszczuk. I am a graphic designer and fine artist who lives in New York City. I find it interesting to work both with my hands and on computer design. My fine art background makes my graphic design projects truly original.

I come from a unique background - I grew up in Japan and came to the United States for my college education (I speak both Japanese and English fluently). Because of this, I see cultural differences between Japan and the U.S. clearly; I often find my perspective different from others.

I have an associate's degree in Fine Art and a Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design. I have a broad range of experience from working as a freelancer, for a branding company, a college, volunteer group, and others. As a graphic desinger, one of the most rewarding moments is when I see printed, finished products of my own. All of the hard work and struggle makes for some of the most fulfilling moments of my life.

I am an ambitious, conscientious, and highly-detailed multi-tasker, who is a great listener, patient, and responsible.

I am interested in in-house graphic design positions with a focus on print-based or packaging design.
